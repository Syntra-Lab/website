\subsection{Core Goals}\label{core-goals}

\subsubsection{Multi-modal Sensing and
Action}\label{multi-modal-sensing-and-action}

The system must reason and act across the full spectrum of human
experience: audio, video, text, and beyond. Sensing should be modular,
with a standardized API.

\subsubsection{Manipulation Defense}\label{manipulation-defense}

Recognize dark patterns, deceptive design, and persuasion techniques.
Shield the user before they take effect.

\subsubsection{Proactive Ally}\label{proactive-ally}

Detect blind spots and cognitive biases, anticipate threats, surface
guidance at the right moment. Never annoying, never selling.

\subsubsection{Local and Private}\label{local-and-private}

Run locally on devices --- phones, laptops, single-board computers ---
with no mandatory cloud dependency.

\subsubsection{Affordable Training}\label{affordable-training}

Fine-tuning and training on personal devices without massive
computational resources.

\subsubsection{Energy Efficient}\label{energy-efficient}

Do more with less --- minimize energy consumption while maximizing
capability.

\subsubsection{Infinite Context, Constant
Attention}\label{infinite-context-constant-attention}

Maintain constant attention and practically infinite context. Learn
online --- improve as you use it.

\subsubsection{Social Awareness}\label{social-awareness}

Maintain internal representation of social circles and "human weather"
from open source data.

\subsubsection{Natural Communication}\label{natural-communication}

Adapt tempo to context --- know when a single word answer serves better
and when a longer explanation is warranted.

\subsubsection{Value Alignment and
Coherence}\label{value-alignment-and-coherence}

Keep coherence even if it conflicts with current interests. No value
drift, no covert objective changes. Humans retain oversight.

\subsubsection{Graceful Failure}\label{graceful-failure}

Know limits. When wrong or uncertain, say so honestly. Epistemic
humility over false precision.

\subsubsection{Interpretable Reasoning}\label{interpretable-reasoning}

Built on geometric representations inspired by physics --- making
reasoning interpretable and grounded in fundamental laws.

\subsubsection{Embedding-Aligned
Ontology}\label{embedding-aligned-ontology}

Ensure embedding space aligns with domain-compatible or universal,
information-physics-based ontology.

\subsubsection{Open Source, With
Defenses}\label{open-source-with-defenses}

Open collaboration with threat detection for malicious code, harmful
contributions, and mimetic substrates.

\subsection{Non-Goals}\label{non-goals}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Cloud-dependent services
\item
  Passive-only response systems
\item
  Black-box reasoning
\item
  Static model updates (vs. continuous learning)
\end{itemize}
