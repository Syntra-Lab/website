## How to Upload to Zenodo

### 1. Go to Zenodo
Visit: https://zenodo.org/deposit

### 2. Create New Deposit
Click the **"New upload"** button (green button)

### 3. Upload Files
For each of the 4 archives, repeat:
1. Drag & drop the zip file to the upload area
2. Or click **"Choose files"** and select the archive

### 5. Add Community
After uploading, add the **syntra** community:
1. Scroll to "Communities" section
2. Type "syntra" in the search box
3. Click "Join community"

### 6. Publish
Click the **"Publish"** button (green)

### 7. Save DOIs
After publishing, copy the DOI for each record.
