\subsection{Why Scholar Exists}\label{why-scholar-exists}

Agency should not be a luxury. The ability to act with clarity, free
from manipulation, informed by intelligence that works for you --- this
should be available to everyone at a reasonably cheap and fair price.
Syntra exists to build a system that serves the community and becomes
sustainable and self-sustaining.

\subsection{The Problem Space}\label{the-problem-space}

The internet is full of systems designed to exploit human psychology ---
dark patterns, deceptive design, engagement traps, and persuasion
techniques engineered to override your judgment. Conventional AI systems
are often:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Server-dependent (can be taken away, surveilled, or manipulated)
\item
  Passive (only responds when asked)
\item
  Opaque (uninterpretable reasoning)
\item
  Expensive to train and run
\item
  Vulnerable to value drift and manipulation
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Toward a World Model}\label{toward-a-world-model}

The ultimate goal is a system that models the world --- not as a passive
repository of facts, but as an active understanding that evolves with
experience. A world model that makes cognition efficient, predictable,
and interpretable --- and potentially offers deeper insights into the
nature of mind itself.
